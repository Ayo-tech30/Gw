Getting Started with NexHost

Welcome to NexHost — the fastest way to host your WhatsApp bots. Follow this guide to go from zero to live in under 5 minutes.

Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and password. You'll receive 10 free starter coins credited immediately to your wallet.

Step 2: Add Your Bot

Navigate to "Add Bot" in your sidebar. Enter your WhatsApp number with the full country code (e.g. 2348056473526 for Nigeria).

Format: [country_code][number]
Nigeria example: 2348056473526
UK example: 447911123456
USA example: 12125551234

Step 3: Choose a Plan

Select Starter (free), Basic (2 coins/day), or Pro (5 coins/day). Coins are deducted from your balance daily.

Step 4: Monitor Your Bot

Use the Statistics page for real-time data, message counts, server load, and uptime monitoring.
